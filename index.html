About

I’m Kaiden, creative polymath working across art, STEM, and writing projects.

Independent creator and systems thinker working across research, technology, and digital art. Develops original frameworks exploring perception, structure, and layered systems, while also building games, tools, and visual projects that bring those ideas into practice. Experienced in both analytical and creative environments, with a focus on clarity, adaptability, and execution. Combines structured thinking with hands-on making, approaching each project with curiosity, precision, and a drive to turn complex ideas into real, working outputs.

2938 Atlantis

A fast-paced science fiction novella set in a future where Earth faces collapse from a devastating virus. Following humanity’s desperate escape to the starship Atlantis, the story explores survival, trust, and the fragile line between hope and horror. Blending dystopian tension with speculative imagination, 2938: Atlantis earned national recognition and the Gold Innovation & Creativity Award at the Engage National Awards 2019.

The "Gen Z Stare" A Cultural & Psychological Phenomenon

In this paper, I look at the "Gen Z stare" as a novel nonverbal behaviour emerging from internet culture and increasingly recognised in offline social interactions, I define it as a prolonged, neutral, wide eyed gaze with minimal affect in contexts where expressive response is socially expected.
